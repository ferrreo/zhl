TextMate reference grammars in this directory are extracted from `@shikijs/langs` 4.3.0.

The `asm`, `arm`, `x86`, `x86_64`, `js-regexp`, `less-bridge`, and
`elixir-text` grammars are
project-authored grammar data for external TextMate scopes. They are
distributed under the repository BSD-3-Clause license.

Source package: https://github.com/shikijs/shiki/tree/main/packages/langs
Package license: MIT

The `coffeescript` grammar is converted from `atom/language-coffee-script`
commit `0f6db9143663e18b1ad00667820f46747dba495e`, MIT licensed.

The `ignore` grammar is copied from Microsoft VS Code commit
`7207f731a477434811e61ca70e6c66ee4dc393fd`, MIT licensed.

The `groovy` grammar is converted from `textmate/groovy.tmbundle` commit
`85d8f7c97ae473ccb9473f6c8d27e4ec957f4be1`.

The `julia` grammar is converted from `JuliaEditorSupport/atom-language-julia`
commit `25cc285b5e8accab4ff7725eeb8594f458b45ce4`, MIT/Expat licensed,
Copyright (c) 2015.

The `lua` grammar is converted from `LuaLS/lua.tmbundle` commit
`b295d83bf0e91b5d3a69eb097f9ed351623b95be`, MIT licensed,
Copyright (c) 2022 最萌小汐.

The `sassdoc` and `scss` grammars are converted from `atom/language-sass`
commits `303bbf0c250fe380b9e57375598cfd916110758b` and
`f52ab12f7f9346cc2568129d8c4419bd3d506b47`, MIT licensed.

The `swift` grammar is converted from `jtbandes/swift-tmlanguage` commit
`3fca2fa10f7dc962d19ee617b17844d6eecfa2cb`, MIT licensed.
